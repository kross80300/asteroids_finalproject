In order to run the game, open Game1.cs in your IDE and hit Run.
Press ENTER to begin the game and use the WASD keys to move. To shoot, press SPACE.
To pause the game hit F.
To enter your initials at the end of the game just type your 3 letter initials on the keyboard and press ENTER to submit.
Press R to restart the game after you have entered your initials.
Press ESC to exit and close the game.
